Master of Geospatial Information Science & Technology 

Its purposes are to showcase a sample of some of the work I completed as part of NC State University's Master of Geospatial Information Science & Technology (MGIST) program of the Center for Geospatial Analytics, College of Natural Resources in Raleigh, North Carolina. 
Additionally, I provide a reflection of my overall experiences in the program and of my Capstone Project.
​
​
The MGIST program is dedicated to providing students with advanced knowledge of Geographic Information Sciences as well as practical applications of technology in analysis, data management and integration, visualization and cartography, and web applications and services.  The program is recognized by the Council of Graduate Schools as a Professional Science Master's degree (PSM).
