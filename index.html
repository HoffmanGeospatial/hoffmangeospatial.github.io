Welcome



Thank you for checking out my website.
The purposes for which I have built this site are to attempt to showcase the technical and conceptual knowledge, skills, application experience, proficiencies, and quality of work of both my formal and informal studies in GIS and Geospatial Analytics, as well as to concurrently document my continued progression and achievements, provide a depository for my own reporting and recording of exercises/tutorials/projects/case studies, and challenge myself to relearn and stay current on the complex coding and programming languages involved - HTML[5], CSS, JavaScript - in creating and styling a space of my own conceptualization that represents and reflects me.

So again, I appreciate you taking the time to peruse my work.
